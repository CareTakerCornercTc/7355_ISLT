About Caretakers Corner

By: Elizabeth Schilling


Have you had a loved one unexpectedly become disabled? I did.



There were so many things that I didn’t know or understand. In September, 2019, my mom and dad suffered strokes on the same day. I discovered that I was completely unprepared for this event. I was very close to my parents. My dad came home from the hospital and died a month later. My mom was still in the hospital. As a result of the stroke, my mom went from being a mobile, lively individual to a paralyzed, bedridden individual. She lost her husband of 60 years and her ability to care for herself.


I hope that by telling our story, it will help you through the challenges you might experience as a person who has been suddenly thrown into a similar life-changing event.


“Caregiving often calls us to lean into love we didn’t know possible.” - Tia Walker
