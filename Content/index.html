Welcome To Caretakers Corner

By: Elizabeth Schilling


Have you had a loved one unexpectedly become disabled? I did.



I created this educational website to help others who are going through the same thing. I hope to help caretakers and family members of stroke victims or other health-related traumatic events by providing educational resources to them in their time of need.


I hope that by telling our story, it will help you through the challenges that you might experience as a person who has been suddenly thrown into a similar life-changing event. You are not alone in this time of struggle!


“Caregiving often calls us to lean into love we didn’t know possible.” - Tia Walker
